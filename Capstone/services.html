Our Services

2nd Level Decks

Second level decks can be tricky. Due to the liability, hiring the right company to build your deck is the most important step. When the safety of your family is concerned, our background in structural framing means your deck doesn't just meet the standards of the building code, it exceeds it.

Stairs and Steps

Stairs and steps can be as basic or as complex as you desire. They are a great way to add the wow factor that everyone is looking for. Building all our stairs from scratch, enables us to customize them to fit your space and needs without loosing the aesthetics of the project.

Roofs and Pergolas

Roofs and Pergolas allow you to enjoy your deck more often throughout the year. Wether you're looking to get out of the sun or stay outside while its raining, we can help you find the solution that fits your lifestyle.

Composite Decking

There are many different types of composites on the market. We can offer a full composite look or a composite/wood blend (all deck boards are composite and the rest is wood). The composite/wood option can significantly reduce the overall cost of the deck while giving you the longevity of the composite deck board.

Railings and Privacies

Railings and/or privacies are code for any decks greater than 2ft off the ground. There are many factors that determine the style of railing or privacy that you are permitted. Multiple options are available, so ensure to choose for both functionality and aesthetics.

Outdoor Living

Outdoor living spaces are simply the best way to increase the quality of your life. Covered outdoor spaces are becoming more popular and more requested year after year. Don't let the rain or the blistering sun deter you from enjoying the fresh outdoors.

Fencing

The requirements for the type and height of fencing varies from one municipality to the next. It is important to know the municipal bylaw before erecting any fencing. Get your fence built right the first time to save money and have it last the test of time.

Multi-Level Decks

Multi level decks can be complicated. The good news is Griffin Decks does not charge extra for multi level decks. Griffin Decks can design and construct a multi level deck based on your available space to meet your lifestyle needs.
